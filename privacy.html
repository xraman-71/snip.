2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. Snip is a fully client-side application. This means:

  • No account creation is required.
  • No login credentials are stored.
  • No email addresses, names, or identifiers are collected.
  • No IP addresses are logged.
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are transmitted.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

When you shorten a URL, the mapping between your short code and the original URL is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This storage:

  • Exists only on your device.
  • Is never transmitted to any server.
  • Cannot be accessed by us or any third party.
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

4. Cookies

Snip does not use cookies of any kind. We do not use tracking cookies, session cookies, or advertising cookies. The only browser storage used is localStorage to save your link history locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Snip loads Google Fonts (Poppins and JetBrains Mono) from Google's CDN. This means your browser may make requests to Google's servers to download these fonts. Google's privacy policy governs those requests. We do not share any user data with Google or any other third party.

We recommend consulting Google's Privacy Policy if you have concerns.

6. Children's Privacy

Snip is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from children. Parents or guardians with questions are welcome to contact us.
